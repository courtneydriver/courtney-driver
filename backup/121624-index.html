Nice to Meet you

Over the last 15 years I've cultivated a keen sense of storytelling across digital, print, AV and experiential mediums. I also helped build and direct one of the most popular luxury publications online from scratch to millions of readers. Creating and directing digital content and campaigns for global brands, and nurturing a stable of nearly a hundred writers and creatives around the world has made it a fun decade and a half.

Running creative and editorial direction around so many household names, I've learned a few things. I thrive being nimble and artistic. I love to communicate, connect and build relationships—both with my team and my audience. And I really love sharing my experience to help solve problems and make magic happen. But don't take my word for it, ask any of the brands that I have worked on or with.
