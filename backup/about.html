About



Over the last 15 years I've cultivated a keen sense of storytelling across digital, print, video and experiential mediums. I also helped build and direct the most popular luxury publication online, creating and directing digital content and campaigns for global brands (and millions of readers), while nurturing a stable of nearly a hundred writers around the world. It's been a fun decade and a half.


Running copy, creative and editorial direction around so many household names, I've learned a few things. I thrive being creative. I love to communicate and connect—both with my team and my audience. And I really love sharing my experience to help solve problems and make magic happen. But don't take my word for it, ask any of the brands that I have worked on or with, down below.
